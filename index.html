About

I am a PhD candidate in Education at the University of Bristol. My work sits at the intersection of educational technology and language learning, with a particular focus on how emerging technologies can be meaningfully integrated into real teaching and learning contexts.

My doctoral research investigates how Virtual Reality Digital Content Creation can support vocabulary consolidation and learner engagement.

More broadly, I study technology-enhanced language learning, AI-assisted language learning, computer-assisted language learning, computer-supported collaborative learning, and learning design.
